Warranty Policy

Standard Warranty

All products include a 1-year manufacturer warranty from the date of purchase.

What's Covered

  • Manufacturing defects
  • Component failures under normal use
  • Hardware malfunctions

What's Not Covered

  • Physical damage or abuse
  • Liquid damage
  • Unauthorized modifications or repairs
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Software issues (unless hardware-related)

Making a Warranty Claim

  1. Contact our support team with your order number and issue description
  2. Provide photos or videos of the defect if requested
  3. Receive return authorization (RA) number
  4. Ship the product back to us with the RA number
  5. Receive replacement or repair within 7-10 business days

Extended Warranty

Extended warranty options are available for select products. Contact us for details.
