How to Shop

Step 1: Browse Products

Navigate through our categories or use the search bar to find the products you need. You can filter by price, brand, and category.

Step 2: Add to Cart

Click "Add to Cart" on any product. You can adjust quantities in your cart before checkout.

Step 3: Review Your Order

Click the cart icon to review your order. Check product details, quantities, and total price.

Step 4: Checkout

Proceed to checkout, enter your shipping information, and select your preferred payment method.

Step 5: Payment

Complete your payment securely. We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

Step 6: Confirmation

You'll receive an order confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships.
